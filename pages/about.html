About Us

Prutas is a proudly Filipino-owned fruit store dedicated to bringing the freshest and most flavorful fruits from across the Philippines directly to your table. From sweet Guimaras mangoes and juicy Davao pomelos to fresh bananas from Bukidnon and lanzones from Camiguin — we celebrate the richness of our local harvest. We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy, high-quality fruits at a fair price. That's why we work closely with trusted farmers and local suppliers to ensure our produce is not only fresh, but also sustainably and ethically sourced. Whether you're buying for your family, for business, or for gifting, Prutas offers a wide variety of seasonal and exotic fruits that showcase the best of what the Philippines has to offer. Thank you for choosing Prutas — your neighborhood fruit store committed to freshness, quality, and the vibrant flavors of the Philippines!
